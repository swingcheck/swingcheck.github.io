FAQ

Questions & answers

Is SwingCheck really free?

+

Yes, completely free. No account, no credit card, no hidden fees. The app runs entirely in your browser using your device's processing power.

What phone or camera do I need?

+

Any phone camera works — iPhone, Android, whatever you have. Just film your swing in decent lighting. Slo-mo mode helps but isn't required.

How accurate is the analysis?

+

SwingCheck uses Google's MediaPipe to detect 33 body landmarks per frame. Metrics like tempo and swing plane are quite reliable. Rotation angles are estimated from 2D video and are best used for relative comparison, not absolute measurement.

Does it work on desktop?

+

Yes — it runs in any modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) on both mobile and desktop. Upload a video from your phone's camera roll or from your computer.

What is ghost mode?

+

Ghost mode overlays a pro golfer's real skeleton data onto your swing replay. It scales to your body proportions so you can see exactly where your positions differ at each phase of the swing.

Is my video uploaded anywhere?

+

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your video never leaves your device — all pose detection and analysis happens on your phone or computer.
